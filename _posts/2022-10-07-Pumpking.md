## Game description
Local multiplayer game where you have to chase a friend and run away from him.
The player with the red light is the chaser, while the one with the yellow one is the escapee.
Each match consists of two rounds of 30 seconds, at the end of each round the roles are swapped, and you get a point if you caught your friend / if you successfully escaped from him.


There are 2 characters, each one with a unique ability:
* the scarecrow can push/pull his opponent depending on his role
* the pumpkin can dash to escape or chase the scarecrow.

* carnivorous plants stop you for a while instead
